Quick Save Bookmarklet

Save any page to only.link with a single click from your bookmarks bar.

📌 Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

+ only.link
👆 Drag the button above to your bookmarks bar
Then click it on any page you want to save!

How to install

  1. Show your bookmarks bar (if hidden):
    • Chrome/Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
    • Firefox: Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
    • Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar
  2. Drag the "+ only.link" button above to your bookmarks bar
  3. Done! Now visit any page and click the bookmarklet to save it

How to use

  1. Navigate to any webpage you want to save
  2. Click the + only.link button in your bookmarks bar
  3. A popup will open with the page details pre-filled
  4. Add tags, adjust the title, and save!
💡 Pro tip: Select text on the page before clicking the bookmarklet - it will be used as the description!
